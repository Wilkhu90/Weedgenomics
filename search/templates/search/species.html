Searchable transcriptome databases were derived from weed species sequenced at Auburn University in collaboration with other university partners. Other sequenced transciptomes were derived from the NCBI Transcriptome Shotgun (TSA) Assembly Database (https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/genbank/tsa/) using the Sequence Set Browser (https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/Traces/wgs/?term=tsa). Sequences acquired from TSA were annotated using Trinotate by first assigning a fake Trinity assembly identifier and using the autotrinotate script. Full credit is given to the authors of the submitted NCBI submitted transcriptomes. Unless otherwise noted, RNA was extracted and sequenced from a developing leaf.
